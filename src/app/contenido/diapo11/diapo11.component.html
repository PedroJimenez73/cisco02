10Refer to the exhibit. The New York router is configured with static routes pointing to the Atlanta and Washington sites. Which two tasks must be performed so that the Serial0/0/0 interfaces on the Atlanta and Washington routers can reach one another?

S0/0/0: 2012::1/126

Loopback1: 2000::1/128

S0/0/0: 2012::2/126

S0/0/1: 2023::2/126

Loopback2:2000::2/128

S0/0/0: 2023::3/126

Loopback3: 2000::3/128

Solution:

Configure the ipv6 route 2023::/126 2012::2 command on the Valencia router

ipv6 route 2023::/126 2012::2 2012::2/126 s0/0/0


Configure the ipv6 route 2012::/126 2023:2 command on the Madrid router

ipv6 route 2012::/126 2023:2 2023::2/126 s0/0/0
